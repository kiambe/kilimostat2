Impacts of Covid 19 0n Food Security

According to the “Survey on Socio Economic Impact of COVID-19 on Households Report” carried out by KNBS in June 2020, nationally, 78.1 per cent of the households reported having enough food stocks (KNBS, July 2020)..

Locust Invasion and Control

Invasion of the desert locust in Kenya has become the worst in the last 70 years. This has caused a threat to food security for nearly 3 million Kenyans.

Fall Army Warms

In Kenya, the pest was first reported in March 2017 in Western Kenya and has since spread countrywide. The pest attacks a wide range of crops but maize and sorghum are the most preferred hosts.
